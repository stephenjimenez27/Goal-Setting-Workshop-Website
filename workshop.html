    SUMMARY

    Our dry-run documentation went well and we were able to present our workshop via zoom meeting and most of the participants enjoyed our workshop, they even said that our topic helped them to realize the importance of goal setting and having a goal. That's also the day we conducted our usability test and most of the participants are looking forward that someday our workshop will improve for the better. Majority of the audience as well are expecting that the flow of the speakers and how the presenter discuss the topic will improve, they also expect that there will be more activities and the audience will participate more.

    GOALS

    The reason why we conducted usability test after we perform our workshop is to know the reactions of the audience and distinguish the improvements that we are going to take in order to make our workshop better, and lastly is to identify if they enjoyed participating in our workshop and the things that they have learned.

    Part 1: Methodology

    1. We did our workshop outline that will determine the timeline or the overview of our workshop.
    2. We conducted the questions that we will use for the usability test using the google forms.
    3. We did our presentation that we will use for the day of our workshop using the gslides.
    4. We did our registration form using the gsheets and then we searched for volunteers who wanted to attend our workshop.
    5. We prepared our invitation card and sent it to those people who wanted to join our workshop.
    6. We performed our workshop with the participants, after we perform our workshop we sent our usability test to their emails.
    7. We informed them to answer our usability test.
    8. We gathered their responses using the gsheets to make it look nice and organized.

    Findings

Also, almost all of them were helped in setting their goals and to know about more goals. It helped them specify what goals they wanted to do first before they do their other goals. We learn a lot especially the things we don't know yet it helps a lot to improve ourselves and we know we have a lot to discover.
