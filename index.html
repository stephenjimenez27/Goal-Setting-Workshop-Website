    OUR OBJECTIVES

    Objective 1
    Objective 1

    Understand the definition of the word "goal" and "goal setting". Also, identify the importance and purpose of goal setting.

    Objective 2
    Objective 2

    Recall the "SMART" criteria and the definition of a smart goal.

    Objective 3
    Objective 3

    Enhance their ability to set their own goals and use the "SMART" criteria.

    Objective 4
    Objective 4

    Make a plan to achieve their own goals and distinguish the different kinds of goals.
