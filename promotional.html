WORKSHOP OVERVIEW

Our workshop is created to promote self-reflection, regulation, development, and management, and this workshop is composed of one presentation and three activities. These activities will allow the participants to socialize or communicate with each other while also teaching them how to associate or engage themselves with other people. This workshop is for those people who want to learn more about goal setting and participate in our workshop. This website is structured to advertise our workshop.

BENEFITS

1. They will learn more about goal setting.
2. They will understand how to set their goals properly.
3. They will know how to achieve their goals correctly.
4. They will recognize what goals to prioritize and accomplish first.

OBJECTIVES

1. To teach people more about goal setting.
2. To help the participants recognize their goals in life.
3. To enhance their abilities when it comes to setting their own goals.
4. To help them make an action plan that could help them achieve their goals.

GOALS

1. To inspire them to fulfill their goals.
2. To find out what they need to improve.
3. To teach them on how to organize their time and what they should prioritize first.
4. To encourage them to make goals.
